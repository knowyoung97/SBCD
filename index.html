Our Story

From Chef Nam's vision to your table

Chef Nam Kyoung Soo

Our founder started his culinary journey at prestigious hotel chains in Seoul, including the Hilton Seoul, specializing in Italian cuisine. He later joined Todai USA and Singapore, one of the world's leading seafood buffet concepts.

In 2016, Chef Nam returned to his culinary roots and founded SBCD Korean Tofu House, bringing authentic, wholesome Korean cuisine to Singapore. With nearly two decades of experience in fine dining, he brings uncompromising standards to every dish.

Soontofu — The Signature Dish

Soontofu means "soft tofu" in Korean — a humble comfort food elevated to fine dining. Our version is meticulously crafted: soft tofu is hand-made fresh daily from specially imported soybeans from Paju, South Korea, a region renowned for pristine agriculture near the DMZ.

The 10-ingredient secret broth took Chef Nam 8 years to perfect. It's boiled and left to set for days to extract natural flavor without artificial additives. This broth is the heart of every soontofu dish — available in four spice levels from mild to crazy hot.

Quality Without Compromise

Every ingredient matters. From Paju-sourced soybeans to premium meats and fresh seafood, we maintain the highest standards. Chef Nam personally oversees ingredient sourcing, menu selections, and cooking techniques, ensuring consistency across all SBCD locations.
